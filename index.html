Hand Wash vs. The Machine

The truth about automatic car washes — and why hand detailing wins every time

🏭

Automatic Car Wash

  • ⚠️ Dirty brushes scratch your clear coat
  • 💧 Recycled water = grit on your paint
  • 🧽 Harsh chemicals strip wax & sealants
  • ⏱️ 3-minute rush job — misses everything
  • 🌀 Swirl marks & micro-scratches guaranteed
  • 💸 You pay for "damage + dry" every time
VS
🧼

Power Up Hand Detail

  • ✅ Two-bucket hand wash — zero scratches
  • ✅ Clay bar removes bonded contaminants
  • ✅ Premium wax & ceramic protection
  • ✅ Every inch cleaned by hand, not a machine
  • ✅ Paint correction removes existing swirls
  • ✅ Protection that lasts months, not days

That "new car shine" dealers show you? It's not magic — it's just proper paint care. Things they don't tell you:

🧠

Wax isn't just for looks — it's your paint's sunscreen. Without it, UV rays, bird droppings, and road salt eat through your clear coat.

🧽

Clay bar removes what washing can't — industrial fallout, tree sap, overspray. That "rough" feeling paint? That's contamination, not normal.

💎

Ceramic coating isn't a gimmick — it creates a permanent bond that makes washing easier and protects for years. Best investment for your paint.
